Shipping FAQs

Common questions about delivery and shipping.

How do I know which courier will be used for my order?

The courier assigned to your order will be shown in your order confirmation message. You'll also receive a courier tracking number once your package is picked up.

Can I request a specific courier?

Yes! You can request a preferred courier when placing your order. Just leave a note in the order comments or message us directly. We'll do our best to accommodate your request.

Are shipping fees final at checkout?

Shipping fees shown at checkout are estimates. Final fees are based on your exact location, package weight, and the courier chosen. Any adjustments will be communicated before your order is confirmed.

What if my package is lost or damaged during shipping?

We take photos of every package before shipping. If your order arrives damaged or is lost in transit, contact us immediately and we'll coordinate with the courier to resolve it or arrange a replacement.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! Check our Discounts page for our current free shipping promos. We regularly offer free delivery for orders within Davao City and on weekends.
