Order Processing

All orders are processed within 1–2 business days after payment confirmation. Since we are open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, orders placed at any time of day are received and queued for processing.

  • Orders placed before 12:00 PM are typically processed the same day
  • Orders placed after 12:00 PM are processed the following business day
  • You will receive a confirmation message once your order has been packed and handed over to the courier
  • A courier tracking number will be provided after pickup
🕐

We're Open 24/7

You can place orders any time — day or night. Our team processes orders Monday through Sunday, including holidays.

⏱

Delivery Timeframes

Delivery times vary depending on your location and the courier assigned to your order. The timeframes below are estimates and may be affected by holidays, weather, or courier volume.

Location Estimated Time Courier/s
Davao City (Local) ⏱ 1–2 business days LBC, Ninja Van, Flash Express
Mindanao (Provincial) ⏱ 2–4 business days LBC, Ninja Van, Flash Express
Visayas ⏱ 3–5 business days LBC, Ninja Van
Luzon / Metro Manila ⏱ 4–7 business days LBC, Ninja Van, Flash Express
International ⏱ 5–14 business days FedEx
⚠️

Delivery times are estimates only and are not guaranteed. Peak seasons such as holidays and sale events may cause delays. We will notify you if there are any significant delays with your order.

💰

Shipping Fees

Shipping fees are calculated based on your delivery address, package weight, and the courier used. The estimated fee will always be shown at checkout before you confirm your order.

Location Estimated Fee Note
Davao City (Local) ₱45–₱100 Free on orders ₱500+ FREE
Mindanao (Provincial) ₱100–₱180 Based on weight & courier
Visayas ₱130–₱220 Based on weight & courier
Luzon / Metro Manila ₱150–₱250 Based on weight & courier
International ₱800–₱4,000+ Via FedEx, varies by country
  • Actual fees may differ slightly from estimates due to package dimensions and weight
  • Any fee difference will be communicated before the order is confirmed
  • Shipping fees are non-refundable once the order has been shipped
🎁

Free Shipping

We offer free shipping under the following conditions. Make sure to check our Discounts page for the latest promo codes.

  • Orders of ₱500 or more within Davao City qualify for free shipping — automatically applied at checkout
  • Free shipping every Saturday and Sunday for all Davao City orders — use code WEEKENDSHIP at checkout
  • Occasional free shipping promos for nationwide orders — check our Discounts page regularly
  • Free shipping promos cannot be combined with other free shipping offers
🎁

Weekend Free Shipping

Every Saturday and Sunday, use code WEEKENDSHIP at checkout to get free delivery on any order within Davao City — no minimum spend required!

🌍

International Shipping

We ship internationally through FedEx. International shipping fees and delivery times vary depending on the destination country and the weight of your package.

  • International orders are processed within 2–3 business days after payment confirmation
  • Delivery takes 5–14 business days depending on the destination
  • The buyer is responsible for any customs duties, import taxes, or fees charged by the destination country
  • We are not liable for delays caused by customs clearance
  • International orders cannot be paid via Cash on Delivery (COD)
  • Package insurance is available upon request for an additional fee
⚠️

Please check your country's import regulations before ordering. Some products may be restricted or prohibited in certain countries. We are not responsible for packages seized by customs authorities.

⚠️

Delays & Shipping Issues

While we do our best to ensure timely delivery, there are circumstances beyond our control that may cause delays. Here's how we handle common shipping issues:

  • Courier delays — If your package is delayed beyond the estimated timeframe, contact us and we'll follow up with the courier on your behalf
  • Lost packages — If your package is marked as lost by the courier, we will file a claim and arrange a replacement or refund within 7–14 business days
  • Damaged packages — If your item arrives damaged, take photos immediately and contact us within 48 hours. We will arrange a replacement at no additional cost
  • Wrong address — Please double-check your address before confirming your order. We are not responsible for failed deliveries due to incorrect addresses provided by the customer
  • Failed delivery attempts — If the courier is unable to deliver after 2 attempts, the package will be returned to us. Re-delivery fees will apply

For any shipping concerns, please contact us through our Contact page or email us at support@homekitchenstudio.com.

Still Have Questions?

Our team is available 24/7 to assist you with any shipping concerns.
