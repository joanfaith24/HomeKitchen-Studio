How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect solely to operate our store and provide you with the best possible experience. We do not use your data for anything unrelated to your transactions with us.

  • To process and fulfill your orders and arrange delivery
  • To send you order confirmations, tracking updates, and delivery notifications
  • To respond to your inquiries, complaints, or return requests
  • To verify your identity and prevent fraudulent transactions
  • To improve our products, services, and website based on customer feedback
  • To send you promos, discounts, and store updates — only if you have opted in
  • To comply with legal obligations required by Philippine law
📣

Marketing Messages

We only send promotional messages to customers who have agreed to receive them. You can opt out at any time by messaging us directly or replying STOP to any promotional message.

🤝

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes. We only share your data with trusted partners who help us operate our business:

  • Courier partners (LBC, Ninja Van, Flash Express, FedEx) — receive your name, address, and contact number to deliver your order
  • Payment platforms (GCash, Maya, banks) — process your payment securely on our behalf
  • Government authorities — only when required by law, court order, or legitimate legal process
⚠️

We require all third-party partners to handle your data responsibly and in accordance with applicable data privacy laws. We do not allow them to use your data for their own marketing purposes.

🔒

Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously. We implement appropriate measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

  • Customer data is stored securely and accessible only to authorized personnel
  • We do not store full credit card or debit card numbers
  • Payment transactions are handled by secure, encrypted payment platforms
  • We regularly review our data handling practices to ensure your information is protected
  • In the event of a data breach that affects your information, we will notify you promptly
🔐

Never Share Your OTP

We will NEVER ask for your OTP, password, or full payment details over chat or SMS. If someone claims to be from our store and asks for this information, do not share it and report it to us immediately.

🍪

Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies — small text files stored on your device — to improve your browsing experience. Cookies help us remember your preferences and understand how visitors use our site.

  • Essential cookies — required for the website to function properly (e.g., keeping items in your cart)
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand how customers navigate our site so we can improve it
  • Preference cookies — remember your settings and choices for a better experience

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect some features of our website. We do not use cookies to track you across other websites.

✋

Your Rights

Under the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act 10173), you have the following rights regarding your personal data. You may exercise these rights by contacting us at any time.

👁️ Right to Access

You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you at any time.

✏️ Right to Correct

You can request that we correct any inaccurate or outdated information we have about you.

🗑️ Right to Erasure

You can request that we delete your personal data, subject to legal and operational requirements.

🚫 Right to Object

You can object to the processing of your data for marketing purposes at any time.

📤 Right to Data Portability

You can request a copy of your data in a commonly used, machine-readable format.

⚖️ Right to Complain

You may file a complaint with the National Privacy Commission (NPC) if you believe your rights have been violated.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us via our Contact page or email support@homekitchenstudio.com. We will respond within 15 business days.

🗄️

Data Retention

We only keep your personal data for as long as it is necessary to fulfil the purposes it was collected for, or as required by law.

  • Order and transaction records are kept for up to 5 years for tax and legal compliance
  • Customer account and contact information is retained while you remain an active customer
  • Inactive customer data is reviewed and securely deleted after 2 years of inactivity
  • Messages and support inquiries are kept for up to 1 year for reference

When your data is no longer needed, it is permanently deleted or anonymized so it can no longer be linked to you.

📝

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or business operations. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

  • Significant changes will be announced via our website or through a direct message to affected customers
  • Continued use of our store after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy
  • We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed
📌

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please don't hesitate to reach out to us. We are always happy to explain.

Questions About Your Privacy?

We're happy to explain how we handle your data. Our team is available 24/7 to answer any privacy-related questions.
