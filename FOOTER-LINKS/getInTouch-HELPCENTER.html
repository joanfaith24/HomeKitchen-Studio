Orders & Tracking

How do I place an order?

Browse our shop, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Fill in your delivery details, choose your payment method, and confirm your order. You'll receive a confirmation message once it's placed.

Orders

How can I track my order?

Go to our Track Order page and enter your order reference number. You'll see the current status and estimated delivery time of your package.

Tracking

Can I change or cancel my order?

You can cancel or modify your order within 1 hour of placing it. After that, the order may already be packed and ready for pickup. Contact us immediately via our Contact page.

Orders

I didn't receive an order confirmation. What should I do?

Check your spam or junk folder first. If it's not there, message us with your name and the items you ordered so we can verify it on our end.

Orders

What are your store hours for placing orders?

We are open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week — Monday to Sunday. You can place an order any time, day or night!

Orders

My order status hasn't updated. Is that normal?

Tracking updates may take a few hours to reflect, especially after pickup by the courier. If there's no update after 24 hours, please contact us and we'll check on it right away.

Tracking
💳

Payments

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Cash on Delivery (COD), GCash, Maya, bank transfer, and online payment via Shopee Pay or Lazada Wallet depending on where you ordered.

Payments

Is Cash on Delivery (COD) available?

Yes! COD is available for orders within Davao City and select nearby areas. You pay the courier upon delivery of your package.

COD

I paid but my order wasn't confirmed. What do I do?

Send us a screenshot of your payment confirmation (GCash receipt, bank transfer slip, etc.) via our Contact page and we'll verify it within 1–2 hours.

Payments

Are there any hidden charges?

No hidden charges. The price shown at checkout is the final amount. Shipping fees, if any, are shown before you confirm your order.

Payments
🚚

Shipping & Delivery

How long does delivery take?

Orders within Davao City typically arrive within 1–3 business days. Same-day delivery may be available depending on your area and order time. Check our Shipping Options page for details.

Delivery

Do you deliver outside Davao City?

Yes, we ship nationwide through our partner couriers (J&T, LBC, etc.). Delivery time for provincial orders is 3–7 business days depending on location.

Shipping

How much is the shipping fee?

Shipping fees vary based on your location and the weight of your order. The exact fee will be shown at checkout before you confirm your order. Free shipping promos are available — check our Discounts page.

Shipping

My package was marked as delivered but I didn't receive it.

Check with your neighbors or household members first. If it's truly missing, contact us within 24 hours of the delivery status update and we'll coordinate with the courier immediately.

Delivery

Can I change my delivery address after ordering?

Address changes can only be made within 1 hour of placing your order and before it's been picked up by the courier. Contact us as soon as possible through our Contact page.

Delivery

Which couriers do you use?

We use J&T Express, LBC, and Grab Express for local deliveries. The courier assigned to your order will be shown in your tracking information.

Shipping
↩️

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for items that are damaged, defective, or incorrect. Items must be unused, in original packaging, and with proof of purchase.

Returns

How do I request a return or refund?

Message us through our Contact page with your order number, a photo of the item, and the reason for return. Our team will respond within 24 hours.

Refunds

How long does a refund take?

Once your return is approved and received, refunds are processed within 3–5 business days. GCash and bank transfer refunds may take 1–3 additional banking days to reflect.

Refunds

What items cannot be returned?

Items that are on sale, perishable, or marked as "final sale" cannot be returned. Personal care products and opened items are also non-returnable for hygiene reasons.

Returns

I received a wrong or damaged item. What should I do?

We're so sorry about that! Please take a clear photo of the item and packaging immediately upon receiving it, then contact us within 48 hours via our Contact page. We'll arrange a replacement or full refund at no extra cost to you.

Returns
