Customer Service Representative

📍 Davao City (Remote / WFH) 🕐 Full-time 💰 Negotiable

About the Role

We're looking for a friendly, patient, and reliable Customer Service Representative to be the voice of our online shop. You'll be the first point of contact for our customers — helping them with orders, concerns, and inquiries every day.

What You'll Do

  • Respond to customer inquiries via chat, SMS, or social media in a timely and professional manner
  • Assist customers with order tracking, delivery updates, and estimated arrival times
  • Handle complaints, returns, and refund requests with patience and empathy
  • Coordinate with the warehouse or delivery team to resolve order issues
  • Keep records of customer interactions and follow up when needed
  • Escalate unresolved issues to the appropriate team member

Requirements

  • Good written and verbal communication skills (Filipino and basic English)
  • Patient, empathetic, and customer-first attitude
  • Can work with a smartphone or laptop — comfortable using chat apps
  • Available to work on weekends (we're open 24/7)
  • No experience required — we will train you!
  • Must be residing in or near Davao City

Nice to Have

  • Previous customer service or online selling experience
  • Familiar with Shopee, Lazada, or Facebook Marketplace
  • Fast typer with good attention to detail

Work Schedule

  • Shifting schedule (Morning / Afternoon / Night)
  • Monday to Sunday — rest days to be discussed
  • Work from home setup available

Why Work With Us

  • 🏠 Work from home setup
  • 📱 Load allowance provided
  • 🎓 Full training — no experience needed
  • 🛍️ Employee discount on all products
  • 📈 Growth opportunities
  • ❤️ Friendly, supportive team
