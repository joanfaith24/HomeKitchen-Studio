Why We're Open 24 Hours — and What That Means for You

⏱ 4 minute read

When we first started our little online shop in Calinan District, Davao City, we asked ourselves one simple question: when do our customers actually need us? The answer, it turns out, was: all the time.

"Convenience shouldn't have a curfew."

We noticed that some of our most loyal customers were night-shift workers, parents who only get a moment to breathe after putting the kids to sleep, and students burning the midnight oil. They needed to shop on their schedule — not ours.

The Decision to Go 24/7

It wasn't an easy call. Operating around the clock means more coordination, more staffing, and more responsibility. But we believed then — and we believe now — that being there for our customers whenever they need us is what sets a good store apart from a great one.

We started by extending our hours gradually. First until midnight, then through the early morning. After a few months of testing, we made it official: we are open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, Monday through Sunday.

What This Means for You

  • Place an order at 2 AM and it will be queued for processing first thing in the morning
  • Message us with a question at any hour — our team monitors inquiries around the clock
  • Never feel rushed to place your order before a cutoff time
  • Track your orders and reach customer service even on holidays

💡 Pro tip: Orders placed before 12:00 noon are typically processed the same day. Orders placed in the afternoon or evening are processed first thing the next morning — so you're never waiting long.

Our Promise to You

Being open 24 hours isn't just a business decision — it's a commitment. A commitment to being there when you need us, whether that's to grab something for dinner, order a last-minute gift, or just browse at your own pace without any pressure.

Thank you for being part of our community. We look forward to serving you — any time of day or night. 🙏

5 Ways to Make the Most of Your Order

⏱ 3 minute read

Shopping online should be easy, rewarding, and stress-free. But with so many options and promos flying around, it can be easy to miss out on savings or make a purchase you regret. Here are five smart habits that will make every order worth it.

  1. 1
    Check the Discounts Page Before Checkout Before you confirm any order, take 30 seconds to visit our Discounts page. We regularly post coupon codes, free shipping promos, and BOGO deals that can save you money instantly. Codes like WELCOME100 and WEEKENDSHIP are easy to miss if you don't look!
  2. 2
    Bundle Your Orders to Save on Shipping Instead of placing multiple small orders, try grouping your purchases into one. Orders above ₱500 within Davao City qualify for free shipping — so it pays to plan ahead and add a few extra items to hit that threshold.
  3. 3
    Order on Weekends for Free Shipping Every Saturday and Sunday, use code WEEKENDSHIP at checkout to get free delivery on any order within Davao City — no minimum spend required. Perfect for smaller purchases!
  4. 4
    Double-Check Your Delivery Address This sounds simple but it's one of the most common issues we see. Before confirming your order, make sure your barangay, street, and landmark details are complete and accurate. A complete address = faster delivery and fewer headaches.
  5. 5
    Unbox on Video — Always We pack every order carefully, but accidents happen in transit. Recording a short video as you open your package protects you in case anything arrives damaged. It's the fastest way to get a resolution if there's ever an issue with your order.
💡

Our team is available 24/7 — if you ever have a question before, during, or after your order, don't hesitate to reach out through our Contact page.

How to Track Your Order Like a Pro

⏱ 3 minute read

One of the most common questions we get is: "Where is my order?" The good news is — tracking your order is easier than you think. Here's a complete step-by-step guide so you're never left guessing.

Step 1: Find Your Order Number

Your order number is sent to you in the confirmation message right after you place your order. It looks something like ORD-20260328-001. Keep this handy — you'll need it to track your package.

Step 2: Go to Our Track Order Page

Visit our Track Order page and enter your order number in the search box. Hit the Track button and you'll see the current status of your package right away.

Step 3: Understand the Status Labels

  • Processing — We've received your order and are preparing it
  • Packed & Ready — Your order is packed and waiting for courier pickup
  • Picked Up — The courier has collected your package from us
  • In Transit — Your package is on its way to you
  • Out for Delivery — Your package is with the delivery rider today
  • Delivered — Your package has been dropped off at your address

Step 4: Track Directly with the Courier

Once your order is picked up, you'll also receive a courier tracking number. You can use this on the courier's own website (LBC, Ninja Van, Flash Express, or FedEx) for even more detailed real-time updates.

📱

Tracking not updating? Give it a few hours — couriers sometimes take time to scan packages at each checkpoint. If there's no update after 24 hours, contact us and we'll follow up for you.
